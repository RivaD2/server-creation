About Me

I have over three years of experience in customer and product support and ever since my first job at a startup I became fascinated with troubleshooting and code. I love putting on the Sherlock hat and believe my passion for troubleshooting customer issues across various platforms and in drastically different industries is a valuable strength. I would like to use my experience to produce code that always maintains focus on helping the user allowing them to walk away happy with an good understanding of how to use a product I helped build. My goal is to use my skills as a developer to build sites for companies in 3D printing, media, consumer products, photography, or cybersecurity.
